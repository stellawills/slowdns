# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please keep collaboration respectful, direct, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discuss technical disagreements in good faith
- give actionable feedback
- respect maintainers' scope and security boundaries
- avoid harassment, insults, or hostile personal attacks

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- doxxing or sharing private operational details
- abusive, discriminatory, or threatening language
- repeated bad-faith disruption of issues or pull requests
- pressuring maintainers to disclose sensitive infrastructure details

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, lock, hide, or remove content that violates this
code of conduct and may restrict access for repeated abuse.

## Reporting

If conduct becomes abusive or unsafe, report it privately through the
project's maintainer contact.
